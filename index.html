The Design of Sensorially Holistic Experiences
Research question
How can the application of computer & 3D graphics, motion and extended realities be used by graphic designers to create immersive experiences through sensorially holistic brand systems?
Sub-questions
How can the design of brand identities be more sensorially holistic? What new properties of the brand can be brought to life by engaging more senses than just the visual? How do these technologies change our vocabulary in terms of what we understand as interaction and “visual” language? How will these tools in turn change the role and skill-set of the graphic designer?
